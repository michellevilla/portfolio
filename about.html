My Story

I have a background in what is basically the UX of fashion. For 5 years I did generative research by paying attention to society’s consumptions shifts, to cultural movements, and by interviewing people at events to learn about their needs and expectations in fashion; and thus, find out how these things were defining the future of the industry. In fashion this is known as Trend Forecasting. Since I have a capacity to translate research into visuals, I also worked as a graphic designer for this niche sector for companies in New York City, and for an Italian eyewear conglomerate.
In 2021, my sense of social responsibility and relentless curiosity led me to pursue UX Design. After introspecting and observing the directions in which people are gravitating in regards to life and what it means to take part in it, I realized that there's nothing out there that I would do unless it integrates business with positive human impact.
Through UX, I'm able to leverage my curiosity, creativity, critical thinking, and analytical skills to catalyze user research, visual design, and business goals. I'm especially fond of user research, through which my international experience puts forward empathy, communication, versatility, and social interaction when working with people.
I’m passionate about why people and culture are the way they are, what steps can be taken to creatively challenge the status quo, and how that translates into meaningful visuals and experiences. If your values align with mine, don't hesitate to get in touch for collaborations and job opportunities.
